Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-6 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Absolutely! I work with clients globally through video calls, project management tools, and collaborative platforms. I'm experienced in remote work and ensure clear communication throughout the entire project lifecycle.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in custom PHP development using vanilla PHP and MVC architecture, WordPress development including custom themes and plugins, MySQL/MariaDB database design, and server management with cPanel, Plesk, and Ubuntu. Check out my Tech Stack page for a comprehensive list.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer various support packages including bug fixes, updates, feature additions, and maintenance. We can discuss ongoing support options that fit your needs and budget during the project planning phase.

How do you handle project payments?

I typically work with milestone-based payments. Usually, 30% upfront, 40% at mid-project, and 30% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a custom payment schedule that works for both parties. I accept various payment methods including bank transfers and digital payments.
